About the app

PPL Canada: Ground Oral Prep is a focused study deck for Canadian student pilots preparing for the ground briefing portion of the Private Pilot Licence flight test. Every question is organized by the section structure your examiner will use.

What's inside

  • 200+ questions across five sections: Documents, Aircraft Performance, Pre-Flight Planning, Charts & CFS, Weight & Balance.
  • Real VNC chart excerpts (Muskoka, Oro-Barrie) and a CFS aerodrome page (Toronto City Centre) with pinch-to-zoom.
  • 14 explanation diagrams covering airspace, mixture/EGT, C of G, weather minima, pitot-static blockage, and circuit join.
  • Multiple-choice questions with three plausible distractors per item, plus open-ended questions for aircraft-specific topics.

What it doesn't do

  • No subscription. One-time purchase.
  • No ads, no accounts, no sign-in.
  • No tracking, no analytics, no data leaves your device.
  • Works completely offline.

Accessibility

Full support for VoiceOver, Voice Control, Dynamic Type, Increase Contrast, Differentiate Without Color, Smart Invert, and external keyboard shortcuts. Every chart and diagram has a detailed VoiceOver description written for student pilots.

Disclaimer

Unofficial study aid. Not affiliated with Transport Canada or NAV CANADA. Aviation regulations and aircraft documentation change — always verify every answer against the current CARs, AIP, CFS, and your aircraft's Pilot Operating Handbook before relying on it for an exam or a flight.
