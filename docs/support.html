Frequently asked questions

Does the App work offline?

Yes, completely. There is no network connection at any time. All 200+ questions, charts, and diagrams are bundled inside the App.

Is my progress saved?

Yes, on your device only. Nothing is sent to a server. Deleting the App erases all progress.

Does the App collect any personal data?

No. The App uses Apple's UserDefaults to remember which questions you've answered and bookmarked — that data stays on your device. There is no analytics, no advertising, no account, no sign-in. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Which aircraft is this for?

The App covers the Transport Canada PPL ground/oral test, which applies to any training aircraft. Aircraft-specific questions (V-speeds, fuel capacity, weight & balance numbers) are presented as open-ended so you can practice with your own Pilot Operating Handbook (POH).

Is this App affiliated with Transport Canada or NAV CANADA?

No. It is an unofficial study aid. Always verify every answer against current CARs, AIP, CFS, and your aircraft's POH.
