About Privacy Support

PPL Canada: Ground Oral Prep (the "App") is published by Nilson Lins Jr.

Data we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, store on a server, or share any personal information. It does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or any third-party tracking.

Data stored on your device

Your quiz progress — which questions you've seen, your answers, your bookmarks, and your in-app settings (theme, density, etc.) — is saved locally to your device using Apple's UserDefaults so that closing the App does not lose your work. This data never leaves your device. You can erase it at any time by tapping the menu in the top right of the main screen and choosing "New Session," or by deleting the App.

Network access

The App is fully offline. It does not make network requests.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs.

Children

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any data from any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be posted at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact nilsonljr@gmail.com.
